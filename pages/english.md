    <p class="mission-statement text-left mt-0">
        Open Nederland is <em>the association</em> for organisations and individuals working on 'Open'.
    </p>

    <p>We are a national network of makers, advisors, and entrepreneurs. From Open Source to Open Science, from Open Education to Open Design, and beyond.</p>

    <p>Our goal is to create, use, support, and encourage 'Open' in its many forms for the public benefit. Open Nederland is also the official steward organisation (Chapter) of Creative Commons in The Netherlands.</p>

    <blockquote>
        <strong>Creative Commons</strong> helps you legally share your knowledge and creativity to build a more equitable, accessible, and innovative world. We unlock the full potential of the internet to drive a new era of development, growth, and productivity.
    </blockquote>

    <hr />

    <h3>Structure</h3>
    <p>Open Nederland is a democratic association. Members meetings are organised at least once every year. The board is responsible for the daily operation of the association.</p>

    <h3>Become a member</h3>
    <p>Are you living or working in The Netherlands and interested in one of the domains we work in? You are welcome to join us.</p>

    <p><strong>Please note:</strong> Currently, our events and internal communication are primarily in <strong>Dutch</strong>. However, many of our members are internationally active and fluent in English.</p>
